Jim Collins, a business researcher, examines why some companies transition from good performance to sustained greatness. Based on a five-year study of 1,435 companies, the book identifies key factors including Level 5 Leadership, the Hedgehog Concept, and disciplined culture that distinguish exceptional organizations.
