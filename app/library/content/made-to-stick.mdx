title: "Made to Stick"
---

Chip Heath and Dan Heath, brothers and business researchers, examine why some ideas survive while others die. The book identifies six principles (SUCCESs) that make ideas memorable: simplicity, unexpectedness, concreteness, credibility, emotions, and stories.
